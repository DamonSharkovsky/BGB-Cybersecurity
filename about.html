About SafeGuard Community

SafeGuard Community is a platform dedicated to raising awareness about AI-driven scams and cybersecurity threats in South Africa. Our mission is to empower individuals and communities to recognize, report, and prevent scams by sharing real experiences, verified reports, and expert advice.

Our Goals

  • Provide a safe space for sharing scam experiences and questions
  • Enable fast reporting of new scam threats
  • Educate the public about AI and cybersecurity risks
  • Connect local communities for mutual support

Contact Us

For support, suggestions, or partnership inquiries, please email us at support@safeguard.co.za.

Future Updates

  • Multi-language Support: We are working to make the community accessible in Afrikaans, isiXhosa, isiZulu, and more South African languages.
  • Mobile App: A SafeGuard Community mobile app is in development for both Android and iOS, making it easier to report scams and stay updated on the go.
  • Advanced Scam Detection: Integration of AI-powered scam detection and real-time alerts.
  • Expanded Resources: More educational content, guides, and tools for digital safety.
  • Local Partnerships: Collaborations with local organizations to strengthen community support and outreach.

We are committed to growing and improving SafeGuard Community to better serve and protect everyone in South Africa.
